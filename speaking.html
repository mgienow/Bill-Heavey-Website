Bill Heavey has been in your bathroom for years...

...So why not invite America's Everyman Outdoorsman to speak at your next event?

Bill's tales of misadventure — from heading out for a hunt minus pants, to crafting an emergency winter hat from his daughter’s stuffed dog, to missing his own 50th birthday party because, well, the fish were really biting that afternoon — are even funnier live and in person. Invite Bill to your group’s next awards dinner, fundraiser or other event as a (surprisingly affordable) speaker whose humorous and heartwarming presentation will be sure to make it a truly memorable occasion.
